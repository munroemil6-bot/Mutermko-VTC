Academic Departments

Engineering & Technology

Courses: Motor Mechanics · Electrical Installation · Welding & Fabrication · Plumbing

The Engineering & Technology Department is the largest at Muteremuko VTC, covering four core technical trades that are among the most in-demand in Kenya's growing construction, manufacturing, and transport sectors.

Training is entirely workshop-based, using industry-standard tools and equipment. Learners benefit from hands-on projects that simulate real workplace environments, with instructors who bring practical industry experience.

All programmes are NITA-aligned (GTT I through GTT III) and include mandatory industrial attachment at GTT III level to ensure graduates are employment-ready from day one.

Building & Construction

Course: Masonry

The Building & Construction Department trains skilled masons ready for employment in the booming Kenyan construction industry. With Kenya's rapid urbanisation and housing demand, Masonry graduates consistently find employment in both formal and informal construction sectors.

Trainees learn in a fully equipped masonry yard and participate in real construction projects during their training period. Emphasis is placed on quality workmanship, structural integrity, and site safety.

Graduates can work with construction companies, government agencies, NGOs, or establish their own building businesses in the community.

Beauty & Fashion

Courses: Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy · Dress Making

The Beauty & Fashion Department combines creativity with entrepreneurship. Both programmes are highly popular and produce graduates who are employment-ready — and business-ready — in Kenya's vibrant beauty, wellness, and fashion industries.

The department features a functional training salon and sewing lab, with students working with real clients under instructor supervision. Business training is embedded throughout both programmes to help graduates manage or launch their own enterprises.

School uniforms for Muteremuko VTC are made in-house by Dress Making students — a testament to the quality and skill level achieved.

ICT & Computer Studies

Courses: Computer Studies (ICT) · Computer Packages (short course)

The ICT & Computer Studies Department equips trainees with the digital literacy and computing skills demanded by today's employers. The department serves both full-programme trainees pursuing NITA certification and short-course students taking the popular Computer Packages programme.

Training takes place in a fully equipped computer laboratory with current software. The department provides practical skills applicable across all other departments — ICT skills complement every career path in the modern economy.

The Computer Packages programme (6 modules, 3 months, Ksh 3,000) is available to anyone who wants foundational computing skills without committing to a full-year programme.
