Admission Requirements

Admissions are open. Bring the following documents when reporting:

  • 5 Files, 2 Coloured Passport Photos, Copy of ID / Birth Certificate & Result Slip (where applicable)
  • One (1) ream of photocopying paper and one (1) ream of ruled paper per year
  • One (1) Black book, 5 books (A4 200 pages) and A3 drawing book
  • Uniform: Black skirt for girls / Black trousers for boys; White branded blouse or shirt @ Ksh 1,500 (made at MVTC)
  • Student Fee: Ksh 100/year  |  Internal Exam Fees: Ksh 300/term
  • Other requirements specific to course of choice (advised on admission)
