📋 What to Bring

📄 Documents

  • 5 Files
  • 2 Coloured Passport Photos
  • Copy of ID or Birth Certificate
  • Result Slip (where applicable)

📚 Stationery

  • 1 ream photocopying paper / year
  • 1 ream ruled paper / year
  • 1 Black book
  • 5 books (A4, 200 pages each)
  • 1 A3 Drawing Book

👕 Uniform

  • Girls: Black skirt
  • Boys: Black trousers
  • White branded blouse/shirt — Ksh 1,500 (made at MVTC)

💰 Fees

  • Student Fee: Ksh 100/year
  • Internal Exam Fees: Ksh 300/term
  • Term 1: Ksh 8,000
