Our Courses

8 NITA-certified programmes — 3 months to 1½ years — designed to launch your technical career.

🔧

1. Motor Vehicle Mechanics

Engineering & Technology Department

This programme equips trainees with comprehensive knowledge and skills in vehicle maintenance, engine overhaul, diagnostics, and repair of both petrol and diesel vehicles. Graduates are ready for employment in garages, dealerships, or self-employment.

What You Will Learn

  • Engine overhaul, diagnostics and rebuilding (petrol & diesel)
  • Transmission systems, gearbox, clutch, and differential repair
  • Electrical systems: wiring, battery, starter motor, alternator
  • Braking systems: drum, disc, ABS fundamentals
  • Suspension, steering, and chassis maintenance
  • Workshop safety, tool use, and record keeping
  • Basic entrepreneurship and business skills for garage management

Course Info

DurationUp to 1½ Years
NITA LevelGTT I, II, III
IntakeEvery Term
Industrial AttachmentMandatory (GTT III)
🧱

2. Masonry

Building & Construction Department

The Masonry programme provides trainees with the foundational and advanced skills required for quality construction work. From foundations to finishing, trainees work on real construction projects throughout training.

  • Foundation laying and concrete work
  • Bricklaying and block work techniques
  • Plastering, rendering, and wall finishing
  • Flooring: screeding, tiling, and terrazzo
  • Reading and interpreting construction drawings
  • Scaffolding erection and safety compliance
  • Estimation of materials and costing

Course Info

DurationUp to 1½ Years
NITA LevelGTT I, II, III
IntakeEvery Term
Industrial AttachmentMandatory (GTT III)
✂️

3. Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy

Beauty & Fashion Department

A comprehensive programme blending practical hairdressing techniques with modern beauty therapy. Trainees graduate ready to work in salons, spas, or start their own beauty businesses.

  • Hair cutting, styling, and perming techniques
  • Chemical treatments: relaxing, colouring, highlighting
  • Skincare: facial treatments, cleansing, toning, moisturising
  • Nail care: manicure, pedicure, nail art
  • Makeup artistry and bridal packages
  • Salon hygiene, sterilisation, and client care
  • Salon business management and marketing

Course Info

DurationUp to 1½ Years
NITA LevelGTT I, II, III
IntakeEvery Term
Industrial AttachmentMandatory (GTT III)

4. Plumbing & Pipe Fitting

Engineering & Technology Department

Plumbing and Pipe Fitting covers installation, maintenance, and repair of water supply, drainage, and sanitation systems in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

  • Cold and hot water supply system installation
  • Drainage and sanitation pipework
  • Pipe jointing: soldering, threading, compression, push-fit
  • Bathroom and kitchen fitting installation
  • Basic gas fitting and solar water heater installation
  • Fault diagnosis and repair of plumbing systems
  • Reading plumbing blueprints and schematics

Course Info

DurationUp to 1½ Years
NITA LevelGTT I, II, III
IntakeEvery Term
Industrial AttachmentMandatory (GTT III)
⚡

5. Electrical Installation

Engineering & Technology Department

This programme trains competent electricians for domestic, commercial, and industrial installations. Fully aligned with Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS) wiring regulations.

  • Domestic wiring: lighting, power, and consumer units
  • Industrial wiring: 3-phase systems and motor controls
  • Earthing and bonding systems
  • Solar PV installation and basic battery systems
  • Fault finding and circuit testing
  • IEE Wiring Regulations compliance
  • Safety: PPE, lockout-tagout, first aid for electric shock

Course Info

DurationUp to 1½ Years
NITA LevelGTT I, II, III
IntakeEvery Term
Industrial AttachmentMandatory (GTT III)
🪡

6. Dress Making

Beauty & Fashion Department

The Dress Making programme covers all aspects of garment construction from pattern making to finished garments. Trainees develop skills in both traditional and contemporary Kenyan fashion.

  • Taking measurements and body proportions
  • Pattern drafting, grading, and cutting
  • Machine and hand sewing techniques
  • Garment construction: dresses, blouses, trousers, suits
  • Embroidery, appliqué, and decorative techniques
  • Fashion design and trend awareness
  • Setting up and managing a tailoring business

Course Info

7. Computer Studies (ICT)

ICT & Computer Studies Department

Practical ICT skills for the modern workplace and entrepreneurship. The full programme prepares students for NITA certification, while standalone Computer Packages modules are also available.

  • Computer hardware fundamentals and maintenance
  • Microsoft Windows, MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access
  • Internet, email, and online communication skills
  • Basic graphic design and desktop publishing
  • Data entry, spreadsheet management, and reporting
  • Digital safety and cybersecurity awareness
  • Computer Packages (6 modules): available separately at Ksh 3,000 for 3 months

8. Welding & Fabrication

Engineering & Technology Department

A rigorous programme covering all key welding processes and metal fabrication techniques used in manufacturing, construction, and engineering. Graduates can work in fabrication workshops, manufacturing plants, or as independent welders.

  • Arc (MMA), MIG, and TIG welding processes
  • Oxy-acetylene cutting and welding
  • Metal fabrication: cutting, bending, rolling, forming
  • Reading and interpreting engineering drawings
  • Welding inspection and quality control
  • Structural steel work and gates fabrication
  • Safety: protective equipment, fume extraction, fire prevention

IntakeEvery Term
